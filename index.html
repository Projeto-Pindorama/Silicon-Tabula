Welcome to the Silicon Tabula!

"Silicon Tabula" ("The Silicon Tablet" in latin) is a collection of text resources (nicknamed "tabulas") for the Pindorama project.
This website lists every documentation for every subproject.
Everything here is (and will be) under constant development, things will change for better.

What is a tabula?

A "tabula" is the type of documentation mainly used by Pindorama, written in Markdown and converted to HTML via its own tool, called traite.
We have created the idea of "tabulas" intending to provide good textual resources about different matters (mainly related to computing), mostly because of the fact that, although the Internet has a lot of information available, sometimes this information is not in fact interconnected, or is hard to understand, or even is biased/fake, with the objective of defending an idea instead of taking the facts and ethics as their base.
In other words, we want to bring back the light for knowledge, in opposition to the counter-enlightenment and Nietzschean ideas that have been taking over it in the recent decades in the form of elitism, pseudo-intelectualism, hedonism and pseudo-libertarianism (or even plain authoritarianism).

Chip in!

If you are willing to contribute to any tabula, by translating, complementing, or correcting it, go ahead! We are aiming to make Silicon Tabula one of the best resources on the Internet for learning, and we can not do this alone.
We are open for pull-requests at our git repository, as long it's helpful for the project.
